Empowering Futures Through Education – A group of close friends giving back to society by supporting underprivileged students with fees, stationery, and the true essence of education.

About Us

At VIDHAI, we are dedicated to support the education of underprivileged and financially struggling students who excel in academics. We believe that financial constraints should never be a barrier to a student's educational journey. Our team works tirelessly to provide the necessary resources and support, ensuring that bright minds have the opportunity to learn, grow, and achieve their dreams.

Our Mission

At Vidhai NGO, we believe in the power of giving back. We aim to provide sustainable development to those in need through various initiatives, including:

  • Educational support for underprivileged children
  • Medical aid and healthcare services
  • Empowerment programs for women and youth

Vision

Inspired by the words of Swami Vivekananda — "If a child cannot come to education, then education must go to the child." — Vidhai envisions a future where every underprivileged student, regardless of financial hardships, has access to quality education. We strive to break barriers and create opportunities, ensuring that no deserving student is left behind in their pursuit of knowledge and success.
